MOTIVATIONAL QUOTE FROM
"THE LION KING"

"You can either run from the past or learn from it"

Conversation between Simba and Rafiki from THE LION KING

Rafiki hits Simba with a stick on the head!!
S: “What was that for?!!”
R: “Doesn’t matter – it’s in the past”
S: “Yes, but it still hurts”
R: “Ah yes, the past can hurt. But the way I see it, you can either run from it or learn from it”
